About Doodle Baseball

Welcome to the Doodle Baseball game! Originally featured as a Google Doodle for the 4th of July celebration in 2019, this charming game lets you step up to the plate as various food items playing America's favorite pastime.

The controls are simple: just click or tap to swing the bat. Time your swing carefully to hit the ball and score runs for your team. See how many runs you can score against the peanut opponents!

This page embeds the game using an iframe, allowing you to enjoy this classic Doodle directly in your browser. Have fun playing!

Meet the Batters!

Get ready to swing with a delightful lineup of batters, including classics like H-Dog (a hot dog), Power Pop (a popcorn kernel), and Big Red (an apple). Each character brings its own unique charm to the batter's box.

Tips for a Home Run

Timing is everything! Watch the pitcher's wind-up and try to swing just as the ball approaches the plate. Hitting the sweet spot often results in a powerful home run. Don't be discouraged by strikeouts; keep practicing your timing!

Google Doodles like this one are a fun way Google celebrates holidays, anniversaries, and the lives of famous artists, pioneers, and scientists. Enjoy this piece of interactive history!
